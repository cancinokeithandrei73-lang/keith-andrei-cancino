Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from digital attacks and unauthorized access.

  • Network security and firewalls
  • Encryption and secure data storage
  • Ethical hacking and penetration testing
  • Incident response and threat mitigation

Cybersecurity professionals help prevent data breaches, malware attacks, and protect sensitive information for organizations and individuals.
