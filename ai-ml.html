AI & Machine Learning

AI & Machine Learning focuses on creating systems that can learn from data and make intelligent decisions without explicit programming.

  • Supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning
  • Neural networks and deep learning with TensorFlow or PyTorch
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP) applications
  • Automation and predictive modeling

This field is crucial for building smart systems, AI assistants, autonomous vehicles, and recommendation engines.
