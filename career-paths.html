Career Paths in Computer Science

Computer Science opens the door to a wide variety of exciting and high-demand careers. Here are some unique paths you can explore:

Software Developer

Design, build, and maintain applications or software systems for businesses, games, or mobile apps.

Data Scientist

Analyze large datasets to extract insights, create predictive models, and help companies make data-driven decisions.

Cybersecurity Analyst

Protect systems and networks from cyber threats by identifying vulnerabilities and implementing security measures.

AI/ML Engineer

Create intelligent systems and algorithms for machine learning, natural language processing, and artificial intelligence.

UX/UI Designer

Design intuitive interfaces and ensure excellent user experience for apps, websites, and software products.
